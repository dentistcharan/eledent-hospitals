Advanced Endodontics

What is Root Canal Treatment?

Root Canal Treatment (RCT) is a restorative procedure used to save a badly decayed or infected tooth by removing the damaged pulp. At Eledent, we perform microscopic RCTs using high-definition magnification and rotary endodontics. This ensures a precision-led, pain-free experience that effectively eliminates bacteria and seals the canal, preventing future reinfection while preserving your natural tooth structure.

Book Your Pain-Free RCT Today

Signs You May Need a Root Canal

Severe Pain While Biting

Extreme Heat or Cold Sensitivity

Swollen or Inflamed Gums

Darkening of the Tooth

Deep Visible Dental Cavities

Pimple-Like Sores on Gums

Spontaneous Sharp Jaw Pain

Teeth whitening is a professional cosmetic procedure designed to lighten the shade of your teeth and remove deep-seated stains. Restorative Dentistry

What is a Dental Crown?

A dental crown is a custom-made "cap" placed over a tooth to restore its shape, size, and structural strength. At Eledent, we use CAD/CAM technology and premium materials like Zirconia to offer "Same Day Crowns." Our precision-milled restorations match the natural translucency of your teeth perfectly, providing long-lasting protection for teeth that are weakened, broken, or heavily filled.

Get Same-Day Dental Crowns

Signs You May Need a Dental Crown:

Missing One or More Teeth

Difficulty Chewing or Eating

Loose or Uncomfortable Dentures

Bone Loss in the Jaw

Severely Cracked or Broken Teeth

Chronic Dental Infections or Severe Tooth Decay

Oral Surgery

What is Wisdom Tooth Removal?

Wisdom tooth removal is the surgical extraction of the third molars, which often lack enough space to emerge properly. At Eledent, our oral surgeons use 3D CBCT imaging to plan extractions with surgical precision, avoiding sensitive nerves. We prioritise a "painless" approach with sedation options, ensuring the removal of impacted teeth is smooth and the recovery period is quick.

Get Instant Relief from Wisdom Tooth Pain

Signs You May Need Wisdom Tooth Removal:

Sharp Pain Behind the Molars

Swollen, Red, or Bleeding Gums

Persistent Bad Breath or Taste

Stiffness or Pain in the Jaw

Difficulty Opening Your Mouth Fully

Cysts Forming Around the Jawbone

Sudden Overcrowding of Front Teeth

Kids Dental Care

What is Paediatric Dentistry?

Paediatric dentistry focuses on the unique oral health needs of children from infancy through their teenage years. Our "Growing Smiles" wing at Eledent is a dedicated, kid-friendly space designed to make dental visits fun and fear-free. Our specialists focus on preventive care, such as sealants and fluoride treatments, alongside habit-breaking appliances to ensure your child develops a healthy, confident smile.
