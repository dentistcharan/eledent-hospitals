Implants or Dentures — Which Is Right for You?

Factor Dental Implants Dentures
Permanence Fixed, permanent, 15+ years to lifelong Removable; replaced or relined every 5–8 years
Feel & Function Closest to natural teeth; full bite force Good, but less bite force; may move while eating
Bone Health Preserves jawbone Does not prevent bone loss over time
Upfront Cost Higher, from Rs 15,000 per tooth Lower, from Rs 8,000 per arch
Treatment Time Few weeks to a few months Faster, typically 1–3 weeks
Best For Patients wanting a permanent, natural solution Patients prioritising lower upfront cost or faster fitting

Implant-supported dentures combine both — denture affordability with implant stability. Ask about this at your free consultation.
